03 — Why Vividex

What working with us actually means.

01

You talk to the builder

No account managers or sales layers. You deal directly with the person doing the work — faster answers, fewer misunderstandings.

02

Honest about scope

We tell you what's realistic before work starts, not after. Out-of-scope items surface early, not at invoice stage.

03

You own everything

All code, all assets, all accounts. Nothing locked into proprietary systems. What we build belongs to you.

04

Accessible pricing

We're a new practice and price accordingly. If agency quotes have felt out of reach, it's worth sending us the brief.

04 — Process

How a project runs.

Every project starts with a direct conversation — not a sales pitch. We define scope, design for approval, build what was agreed, and ship it.

  1. 01

    Discover

    We map out exactly what needs to be built, who it's for, and what success looks like — before anything is designed or coded.

  2. 02

    Design

    Wireframes and visual designs reviewed and approved before a line of code is written. No surprises at handover.

  3. 03

    Develop

    Clean, maintainable code built to the agreed scope, with progress updates at each milestone.

  4. 04

    Launch

    Deployment, testing, and handover. We remain available after go-live for questions and follow-up work.
