05 — Process

How a project runs.

Every project starts with a direct conversation — not a sales pitch. We define scope, design for approval, build what was agreed, and ship it.

  1. 01

    Discover

    We map out exactly what needs to be built, who it's for, and what success looks like — before anything is designed or coded.

  2. 02

    Design

    Wireframes and visual designs reviewed and approved before a line of code is written. No surprises at handover.

  3. 03

    Develop

    Clean, maintainable code built to the agreed scope, with progress updates at each milestone.

  4. 04

    Launch

    Deployment, testing, and handover. We remain available after go-live for questions and follow-up work.
